PSVK PROPOSED FEATURES OF A PROPER WEBSITE 2026.
1. USER INTERFACE AND EXPERIENCE:
Intuitive Design:
A clean, uncluttered interface that is easy to navigate and understand, allowing users to quickly find and book rooms.
Mobile-Friendly:
Optimized for various devices (desktops, tablets, smartphones) to ensure a seamless booking experience on any screen.
Real-Time Availability:
Displays accurate and up-to-date information on room availability, preventing overbooking and frustration.
Advanced Search Filters:
Allows users to refine their search based on various criteria like price, amenities, location, and more.
High-Quality Images and Descriptions:
Showcases rooms and amenities with detailed descriptions and visuals tours to attract potential guests.
Clear Calls to Action:
Prominent buttons and prompts guiding users through the booking process.
2.

BOOKING AND RESERVATION MANAGEMENT:

Secure Payment Gateway:
Integrates with reliable payment processors to ensure secure and convenient transactions.
Multiple Payment Options:
Offers a variety of payment methods (credit cards, M-Pesa, Cheques, PayPal, etc.) to cater to different preferences.
Automated Confirmation and Notifications:
Provides instant booking confirmations via email or other channels, enhancing customer satisfaction.
Cancellation and Modification Policies:
Allows users to easily cancel or modify bookings, with clear policy information.
Integration with Channel Managers:
Connects to various online travel agencies (OTAs) and distribution channels to manage inventory and pricing.

Guest Profiles and Loyalty Programs:
Saves guest preferences, reservation history, and personal information for personalized experiences.
3.

FUNCTIONALITY AND SUPPORT:

Multi-Language and Currency Support:
Caters to international travelers by offering content and payment options in multiple languages and currencies.
Customer Support:
Provides options for live chat, email, or phone support to address user queries and concerns.
Integration with Hotel Systems:
Connects with other hotel management systems for streamlined operations (e.g., property management systems, revenue
management systems).
Reporting and Analytics:
Offers tools to track bookings, analyze website traffic, and gain insights into customer behavior.
Promotions and Discounts:
Allows hotels to offer special deals and promotions to attract guests.
4. ESSENTIAL EXTRAS:
Guest Reviews: Displays reviews from previous guests to build trust and credibility & client’s subscription button for
hotel’s newsletter.
Hotel Information: Provides comprehensive information about the hotel, including location, amenities, menus, videos
and gallery and contact details.
Social Media Integration: Allows users to easily share their bookings and experiences on social media platforms, as well
as integrate with the Company’s social media platforms, (WhatsApp, Tik Tok, Instagram and Facebook)
Mobile App (Optional): Provides a dedicated mobile app for a more convenient booking experience.
Cloud-Based Services: Offers a reliable and scalable platform for managing bookings and data.

N/B: A hotel website should feature a user-friendly booking engine, high-quality images, mobile-friendly
design, detailed room and amenity information, customer reviews, a contact form, social media
integration, clear calls-to-action, and a multilingual option to cater to international guests.
